Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar. The temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is a sacred pilgrimage town about 70 km from Solapur. It is famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is located about 40 km from Solapur and is famous for Shri Swami Samarth Temple.

Solapur Fort

Also called Bhuikot Fort, it was built during the Bahmani Sultanate period and reflects medieval military architecture.
