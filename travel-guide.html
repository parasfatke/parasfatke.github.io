How to Reach

By Road

Solapur is connected to Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Bengaluru.

  • Pune – 250 km
  • Hyderabad – 300 km
  • Mumbai – 400 km
  • Bengaluru – 380 km

By Rail

Solapur Railway Station connects major cities like Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai and Bengaluru.

By Air

Nearest airports are Pune International Airport and Hyderabad International Airport.

Local Transport

  • Auto Rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City Buses
